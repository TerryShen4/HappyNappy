FILE: mqtt_testing/mqtt_pub.py
ROLE: a TEST tool - a fake pillow (variant). Not part of the live system.

PURPOSE
  Same idea as mqtt/mqtt_pub.py: publishes a synthetic ~70 BPM heartbeat to the
  broker so you can test the backend/dashboard without the physical ESP32.

DIFFERENCES FROM mqtt/mqtt_pub.py
  - Shorter window: 250 samples at 50 Hz (5 seconds) instead of 500 (10 seconds).
  - Includes an extra "finger": true field in the JSON.

WHAT IT DOES
  Generates the fake pulse waveform, wraps it in JSON, publishes it once to
  sensors/max30102/data, then exits.

NOTE
  These two test publishers are leftovers from development; either one can stand in
  for the real pillow when you just want to exercise the software.
