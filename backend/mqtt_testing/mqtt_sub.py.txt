FILE: mqtt_testing/mqtt_sub.py
ROLE: a TEST tool - a "listener" that prints raw MQTT messages. Not part of the
       live system.

PURPOSE
  A tiny debugging helper. It connects to the broker, subscribes to the pillow's
  data topic, and prints whatever messages arrive. Use it to confirm the pillow
  (or a fake publisher) is actually sending data to the broker.

WHAT IT DOES
  - Connects to the broker (localhost:1883).
  - Subscribes to sensors/max30102/data.
  - Prints each incoming message's topic and contents until you press CTRL+C.

WHY IT'S USEFUL
  It checks ONE link in the chain in isolation: "is data reaching the broker?" This
  is exactly the kind of test that, during development, proved the pillow was
  publishing fine and pointed the blame at the backend instead (see the debugging
  story in TECHNICAL_MANUAL.txt).
