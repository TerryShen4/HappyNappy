FILE: processing.py
ROLE: the signal-processing math - turns a raw window of sensor samples into a
       heart rate. Used by api_server.py.

PURPOSE
  Holds the pure "number in, number out" functions, with no networking and no
  saved state. Keeping the math separate makes it easy to read and to test on its
  own.

WHAT'S INSIDE
  average_ir(ir_data)      - the mean of an IR window; used as a quick measure of
                             how strong the signal is.

  has_good_contact(ir_data) - True if the average IR is high enough that a finger
                             is really on the sensor (otherwise the window is
                             ignored).

  calculate_bpm(ir_data, sample_rate)
                           - the core function. It uses the HeartPy library to:
                               1. band-pass filter the wave (keep only heartbeat
                                  frequencies, remove drift and noise),
                               2. find the beats and measure the time between them,
                               3. convert that into beats per minute.
                             Returns the BPM (rounded), or None if the signal can't
                             be turned into a valid number.

DEPENDS ON
  config.py (the filter/contact settings) and the HeartPy library.
