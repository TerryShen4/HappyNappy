FILE: test_MQTT.cpp
BUILD: compiled by the "test_mqtt" PlatformIO environment.
       A TEST/BRING-UP SKETCH - not the full product.

PURPOSE
  A stripped-down program written to prove the MQTT data path worked during
  development. It does the core streaming job and nothing else fancy:
    - connects to WiFi and the MQTT broker,
    - samples the heart sensor and publishes 500-sample windows to
      sensors/max30102/data,
    - listens for the backend's wake/stop commands.

HOW ITS WAKE BEHAVIOR DIFFERS FROM THE PRODUCT
  Its buzzMotor() is a simpler, BLOCKING version: when a wake alert arrives it
  shows an "ALARM! / SNOOZE" screen and vibrates in a loop until you press DOWN on
  the joystick, then shows "Snoozed!". (The product's version uses non-blocking
  vibration patterns and the full menu system instead.)

WHY IT EXISTS
  Used to isolate and verify the networking/sensor pipeline without the full menu
  and alarm-clock code getting in the way. Handy for debugging the MQTT link, but
  it is not the demo firmware - that is the "integrated" build.
