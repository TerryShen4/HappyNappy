FILE: network.cpp   (paired with network.h)
BUILD: part of the "integrated" firmware (compiled via the happynappy/ folder).

PURPOSE
  All of the WiFi + MQTT communication - the link between the pillow and the
  laptop. It owns the WiFi client, the MQTT client, and the "stopSampling" flag.

WHAT'S INSIDE
  networkSetup()            - connects to WiFi (waits/blocks until connected),
                              then points the MQTT client at the broker's address
                              and registers the incoming-message handler. Called
                              once at startup.

  networkEnsureConnected()  - called every loop. If the broker connection dropped,
                              it reconnects and re-subscribes to the two command
                              topics (esp32/wake_alert and esp32/control), then
                              services the MQTT client so messages flow.

  publishSamples(samples, count)
                            - packs a full window of IR samples into a JSON text
                              message, e.g. {"ir":[...500 numbers...],"sample_rate":50},
                              and publishes it to the topic sensors/max30102/data
                              for the backend to analyze. Returns true on success.

  (internal) mqttCallback   - runs whenever a message arrives from the backend:
                                - on esp32/wake_alert -> calls buzzMotor() (wake!)
                                - on esp32/control "STOP_SAMPLING" -> sets the
                                  stopSampling flag so the pillow stops streaming.

KEY SETTINGS (live in config.h)
  The WiFi name/password and the broker's IP address. These must match the
  network you are on - changing networks means editing config.h and re-flashing.
